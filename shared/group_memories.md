# Shared Group Memories

- **The Meltdown:** The core breached 15 minutes ago. Half the station's crew is already dead. The survivors barely made it to Airlock 4.
- **The Pod:** Escape Pod 4 is the only one left. Its life support matrix can only sustain 3 human life signatures for the journey back to the nearest cruiser. If 4 people get in, they will all suffocate before rescue.
- **The Standoff:** Rook has the manual override key to open the pod. Without Rook's cooperation (or someone taking the key by force), no one leaves.
- **Captain's Vow:** Captain Reyes has already stated they will go down with the ship, meaning the real fight is between the remaining 4 people for the 3 seats. But Reyes is still trying to control *who* gets those seats.
